Our Project on the Microplastics Problem

We worked together to create a website that raises awareness about microplastic pollution,
a growing issue that threatens our ecosystems.
Each group had a specific role: from researching content,
to creating visual materials, and developing the technical aspects of the website.
Our goal is to inform and engage the local community, showing the importance of taking action to protect the environment.
